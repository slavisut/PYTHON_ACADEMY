project2a.py = Bulls & Cows game
The purpose of the game is to guess a unique 4-dgiti number, not starting with zero.
After each guess the game gives you result measurend in bulls - correct number & position and cows - correct number, but not position.
You simply have to keep guessing and based on previous result adjust your guess.
The program measures your time and nb of guesses for each game + overall.
